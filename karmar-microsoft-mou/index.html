MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

For Technical Cooperation between KARMAR LLC and Microsoft Corporation

FIRST PARTY:

KARMAR – Limited Liability Company

(KARMAR – LLC)

Headquarters: Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Represented by: Abdulkarim Abdullah Alanazi

Title: Official Representative and Chief Executive Officer

Hereinafter referred to as "KARMAR".

SECOND PARTY:

Microsoft Corporation

Headquarters: Redmond, Washington, United States

Represented by: Kevin Scott

Title: Chief Technology Officer – Microsoft

Hereinafter referred to as "Microsoft".

1 INTRODUCTION

In the mutual interest of both parties to establish a technical and strategic cooperation relationship in the fields of cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and digital platforms, this memorandum has been signed to serve as a general framework for cooperation and knowledge exchange.

2 OBJECTIVES

This memorandum aims to:

  • Develop joint products and solutions based on Azure services and Microsoft technologies.
  • Enable KARMAR to utilize Microsoft's cloud and artificial intelligence services.
  • Support KARMAR's staff with training and technical certifications.
  • Enhance collaboration opportunities in smart cities and modern digital systems.

3 SCOPE OF COOPERATION

The scope of cooperation includes, but is not limited to:

  • Integration of KARMAR systems with Azure platform.
  • Utilizing Microsoft AI and Cognitive Services.
  • Developing artificial intelligence solutions and joint technical projects.
  • Exchanging consultative knowledge and technical support.
  • Regular coordination meetings between technical teams of both parties.

4 OBLIGATIONS

KARMAR Obligations

  • Provide technical and executive team supporting the implementation of cooperation projects.
  • Comply with cybersecurity policies and data protection.
  • Respect intellectual property rights of both parties.
  • Use Microsoft services within agreed limits.

Microsoft Obligations

  • Provide technical support for Azure services and artificial intelligence technologies.
  • Enable KARMAR to access necessary cloud tools.
  • Provide appropriate training and facilitate technical certification paths.
  • Support the development of technical solutions based on Microsoft products.

5 CONFIDENTIALITY AND DATA PROTECTION

Both parties commit to maintaining the confidentiality of exchanged information and not disclosing or using it outside the scope of cooperation, except with written consent from the other party.

6 DURATION

The duration of this memorandum is two years from the date of signing, and shall be automatically renewed or by a new written agreement between the parties.

7 LEGAL NATURE

This memorandum is not a commercial contract and does not impose direct financial obligations. It is a preliminary technical cooperation framework, and binding contracts may be signed later as needed.

8 TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this memorandum with prior written notice of not less than thirty (30) days.
