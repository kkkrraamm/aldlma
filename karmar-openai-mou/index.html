1 INTRODUCTION

This Memorandum of Understanding ("MoU") is established to define a collaborative framework between KARMAR LLC and OpenAI for the purpose of advancing artificial intelligence applications, improving AI-driven solutions, and exploring innovations that leverage OpenAI technologies.

Both parties aim to strengthen research, development, and responsible deployment of advanced AI systems aligned with shared goals of safety, innovation, and positive global impact.

2 PURPOSE OF THE MOU

The objectives of this MoU include:

  • Integrating OpenAI technologies, APIs, and models into KARMAR's platforms and applications.
  • Enhancing KARMAR's AI capabilities through OpenAI's development tools, safety frameworks, and best practices.
  • Supporting innovation in conversational AI, autonomous systems, smart cities, and enterprise solutions.
  • Providing technical guidance to KARMAR regarding model optimization, fine-tuning, and scalable AI deployment.
  • Exploring joint research opportunities in AI safety, advanced reasoning, multimodal intelligence, and developer tools.

3 SCOPE OF COLLABORATION

Collaboration under this MoU may include, but is not limited to:

  • Integration of OpenAI models (GPT, Embeddings, Vision, Audio) into KARMAR systems.
  • Development of custom AI assistants, enterprise automations, and domain-specific solutions.
  • Participation in developer programs, technical reviews, and innovation workshops.
  • Knowledge exchange around safe deployment, alignment standards, and ethical AI use.
  • Performance tuning, prompt engineering, and infrastructure optimization.
  • Exploring opportunities to expand AI services regionally and globally.

4 RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE PARTIES

Responsibilities of KARMAR

  • Provide engineering teams to participate in AI development and integration.
  • Follow OpenAI policies, safety guidelines, and usage standards.
  • Protect confidential data and respect OpenAI's IP rights.
  • Deliver high-quality implementations aligned with best practices.

Responsibilities of OpenAI

  • Provide technical support for OpenAI APIs, models, and tools.
  • Grant access to documentation, platform resources, and relevant technologies.
  • Support research, development, and engineering enablement for KARMAR teams.
  • Offer insights regarding safe deployment, model performance, and responsible AI design.

5 CONFIDENTIALITY

All information exchanged during collaboration shall remain confidential and may not be disclosed without prior written consent, except as required by law.

6 DURATION

This MoU shall remain in effect for two (2) years from the date of signing and may be renewed through mutual written agreement.

7 LEGAL NATURE

This MoU is non-binding and does not create financial or contractual obligations. It represents a high-level collaboration framework that may lead to future binding agreements.

8 TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this MoU with a written notice of at least thirty (30) days, without affecting any rights established before termination.
